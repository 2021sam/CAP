Logging

Repair Centers Repair Order RO is referenced in the log as ro1, ro2, ...
BEAR's vendor invoice is referenced as Invoice.
You can only edit your own transactions.
An RO & invoice are required for a completed valid log.
Once you are done with creating and/or updating your log, you may Close it.

Manual CC Linking

The credit card transactions are automatically imported daily from the credit card bank website.
The credit card log field is linked to a credit card transaction with the following steps:
1. Select a log to Update.
2. Click the Search button.
3. Select the corresponding credit card charge.
4. Click the Update button.

Automatic CC Linking

The credit card transactions are automatically imported daily from the credit card bank website.
The credit card log field is linked to a credit card transaction automatically if the following criteria is met:
1. The log was created no more than 10 days prior to the credit card transaction process date.
2. The amounts match to the penny.
3. There is a matching word in the log & credit card transaction for the vendor name.
